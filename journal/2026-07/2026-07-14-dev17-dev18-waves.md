[FACT 2026-07-14] **LLVM point-release skew broke llvm-seahorn #26 CI**:
noble's archive ships LLVM 18.1.3; the port and its regenerated
sea_instcombine corpus were built against apt.llvm.org 18.1.8. InstCombine
output routes partly through the LINKED LLVM libs (InstSimplify/
ValueTracking), so 18.1.3 folds two tests differently than the 18.1.8 CHECK
lines expect. Fix: CI installs 18.1.8 from apt.llvm.org — corpus and CI must
come from the SAME point release. (Never bit on 16/17 only because local and
CI happened to agree.)
